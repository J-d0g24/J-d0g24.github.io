Leonardo da Vinci Profile

Introduction

Leonardo da Vinci, a polymath of the Renaissance era, is widely recognized for his unparalleled contributions to art, science, and engineering. His innovative thinking and artistic brilliance have left an indelible mark on the course of human history.

Background

Born on April 15, 1452, in Vinci, Italy, Leonardo da Vinci showed early artistic talent. He apprenticed with Andrea del Verrocchio and gained expertise in various fields, from painting to anatomy.

Well Known For

  • Masterful paintings such as the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper
  • Advancements in anatomy and scientific sketches
  • Inventions and designs, including flying machines and war devices

Examples of His Work

  1. The Mona Lisa
  2. The Last Supper
  3. Vitruvian Man
